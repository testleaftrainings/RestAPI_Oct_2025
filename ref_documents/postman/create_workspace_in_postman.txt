Create Workspace in Postman:-

Step 01: Open Postman application
Step 02: Ensure you're logged into your Postman account
Step 03: Click on "Workspaces" in the top navigation bar
Step 04: Select "Create Workspace" from the dropdown menu
Step 05: By default "Blank Workspace" is selected. If not, select the same
Step 06: Click on "Next" button
Step 07: Enter a clear, descriptive name for your workspace
Step 08: Select workspace type as "internal". By default it is selected
Step 09: Select "Only you and invited people" manage access
Step 10: Click on "Create" button
